About MyTeamTV

MyTeamTV started as a simple idea: sports fans shouldn’t need five different apps just to follow their teams. The platform is designed to be a single hub where you pick what you care about and pay for a plan that fits.

Why MyTeamTV Exists

The problem: Fans are forced to juggle multiple subscriptions to watch their favorite teams.
Our answer: A centralized platform with customizable access by sport and team.
The outcome: Lower cost, less friction, and higher fan engagement.

MISSION

Make sports streaming simpler, fairer, and more personal — with one place to watch the teams and sports you actually follow.

VISION

Become the go-to fan-first alternative to expensive bundles by combining streaming, community, and personalization in one platform.

WHAT MAKES IT DIFFERENT

  • Customization: subscribe by teams + sports, not random channels
  • One login: a single home base for your games and highlights
  • Community: team hubs + watch parties to bring fans together
  • Realistic tiers: pricing modeled near what fans already pay today

WHAT’S NEXT

The long-term goal is to expand content partnerships and add premium features like multi-game view, condensed games, and smarter recommendations — while keeping the experience simple and fan-first.
