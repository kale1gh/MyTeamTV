Pricing Strategy

Goal: Maximize adoption while capturing high-value fans.
Approach: Tiered pricing based on sports and teams selected.
Outcome: Predictable recurring revenue with natural upgrade paths.

BASIC FAN

$12.99
1 sport • up to 2 teams
Best for casual fans who follow one sport closely.

TEAM PASS

$24.99
2 sports • up to 5 teams
Great for fans who watch multiple teams across two sports.

ALL-STAR

$34.99
up to 4 sports • up to 10 teams
Built for fans who want more coverage without full Unlimited.

UNLIMITED ACCESS

$44.99
All sports + teams • premium features
Replaces the “subscribe everywhere” experience.
Why tiered pricing works
  • Lower entry cost for casual fans → more sign-ups (volume)
  • Upgrades happen naturally as users add teams/sports
  • Unlimited is a clear “bundle alternative” for power users
