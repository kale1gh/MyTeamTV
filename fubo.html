Strategic Partnership

MyTeamTV x fubo

MyTeamTV partners with fubo to power live sports streaming through an established platform with existing media rights.

Why This Partnership Matters

  • No need to purchase expensive media rights independently
  • Instant access to live games across leagues
  • Reliable, high-quality streaming infrastructure
  • Faster go-to-market strategy

How It Works

Users build their custom MyTeamTV plan, then stream games directly through fubo integration. MyTeamTV enhances the experience with:

  • Team-based dashboards
  • Watch parties + live chat
  • 2K-style fan progression system
  • Personalized schedules + alerts

Revenue Model

  • Subscription bundling with fubo
  • Revenue share agreement
  • Premium fan features (badges, upgrades)
  • Targeted ads + sponsorships
